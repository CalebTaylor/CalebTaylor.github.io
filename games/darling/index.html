A short art/story game.

Was quickly inspired by empathy / love / connection / cooperative based games, and loved the setting of the Kiev Riots. Made a game about both.

Wander through the ruined streets, avoid molotov cocktails, listen to a campfire song, and find your darling.

The rotoscoping I think adds a lot to the feel of the game; a weird, dreamy, dark and unsettling story where everyone looks the same and all you want is a hug.
